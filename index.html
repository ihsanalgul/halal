1. The Mountain Expedition

Themes: Geography, Nature, Appearance, 'Used to'

Last summer, five friends decided to head to the mountains in the north. The group leader, John, was a stocky man in his forties with a thick beard. He was very athletic and experienced. Sarah, who was petite and usually looked very glamorous in the city, looked a bit scruffy in her hiking gear, but she was ready.

They set up their camp in a valley near a cold mountain lake. The surroundings were stunning. John pointed to a high peak. "That summit is our goal," he said. "We have to climb that steep slope."

Tom, another friend, looked nervous. "I used to be terrified of heights," he admitted. "But I want to try."

They started walking along a rocky trail. They passed a dark cave and drank water from a natural spring. It was a difficult climb, far from any populated area. To survive in such wilderness, you need to trust your team. When they finally reached the top, the view of the plain below and the distant ocean coast to the west was incredible.

2. Dinner at "The Spicy Pepper"

Themes: Food, Tastes, Restaurant, Present Continuous vs Simple

Martha and Peter wanted to try a new restaurant. They made a reservation at a place famous for its spicy food.

"Do we have a table?" Peter asked the waiter.
"Yes, right this way," the waiter said, placing a cloth napkin on their laps. "Tonight, the chef's appetizer is on the house. It's a real treat."

He brought a plate of crunchy fried vegetables. "Martha is tasting the appetizer right now," Peter thought. "She looks happy."
"Mmm, this tastes delicious," Martha said. "I love the flavor of the fresh ingredients."

For the main course, Martha, who is a vegetarian, ordered a savory stew packed with cabbage, bell peppers, and beans. Peter, who was very hungry, was ready to devour the daily special: a large steak.

The chef is known to whip up amazing sauces. "Be careful, the sauce is hot!" the waiter warned. It was a fantastic meal. They were so full up that they couldn't eat dessert, not even a small pastry. They paid the bill and left a generous tip.

3. Generations

Themes: Aging, Appearance, Personality, 'Used to'

Arthur is a pensioner (or senior citizen) living in a quiet suburb. He is now an elderly man in his late 80s. When he looks in the mirror, he remembers what he looked like before.

He used to have a head full of dark hair and broad shoulders. But years ago, he started to go grey, and then started to lose his hair. Now, he is quite thin and looks frail. Sometimes he feels tired and gets a bit grumpy about small things, but deep down, he is very sweet.

His granddaughter, Lily, visits him often. She is a twenty-something university student. She is very fashionable, wears her hair in bangs (or a fringe), and has a small tattoo on her arm. She looks very youthful and cute. Arthur loves that she makes an effort to keep in touch. They sit together, and he likes to remember stories from when her grandmother was alive, after they got married.

4. Office Drama

Themes: Relationships, Feelings, Conflict

Clara is a smartly-dressed woman who works in a large company. When a new colleague, Dave, joined her team, she immediately had a crush on him. He was tall, had a pointy nose that she thought was charming, and a great sense of humor.

They soon realized they had a lot in common. They were both into hiking and foreign films. They started seeing each other quietly. It felt like a strong bond was forming.

However, after a few months, Clara heard some colleagues start to gossip. Someone said Dave might cheat on her with someone from another department. Clara felt incredibly depressed. She decided to confront him.

They had a terrible row in the park. "I can't trust you if there are secrets!" she cried. They almost decided to end the relationship right there. But they talked for hours. Dave explained it was a misunderstanding. Eventually, they hugged and made up. They are still a couple and are learning to get to know each other better every day.

The city's main attraction is a dormant volcano that is not expected to erupt again. Many people travel there just to see the volcano's massive crater. The surroundings are very dry, almost like a desert, because it rarely rains.
